Frequently Asked Questions

How are tax brackets applied?

Tax brackets use marginal rates where only income within each bracket's range.

What counts as taxable income?

Includes wages, business income, investments, and retirement distributions.

How does marriage affect taxes?

Filing jointly typically offers lower rates but consider the "marriage penalty".
