Frequently Asked Questions

Q1: Who is considered a filer in Pakistan?

A filer is an individual who appears on the Active Taxpayers List maintained by the Federal Board of Revenue (FBR).

Q2: Are there any benefits for senior citizens?

Yes, citizens aged 60 and above enjoy reduced tax rates or slabs on certain income brackets.

Q3: Does the tax vary based on profession?

Yes, salaried individuals and business owners are taxed under different tax brackets in Pakistan.
